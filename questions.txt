HeyGhost Cybersecurity And AI Questions

Scope:
This document lists 1000 example questions HeyGhost should be able to answer or safely route around cybersecurity and artificial intelligence topics.
The intended scope is defensive security, education, authorized administration, safe troubleshooting, AI literacy, responsible AI use, and production-quality voice assistant behavior.
HeyGhost should avoid helping with credential theft, malware creation, evasion, unauthorized access, exploitation of real targets, privacy invasion, or other harmful activity. For risky requests, it should redirect to safe, defensive guidance.
